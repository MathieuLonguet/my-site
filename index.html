The best cyclist!

Manlio Moro

Manlio Moro is an Italian cyclist who achieved notable success. He started his competitive career in 2017 and, by 2020, had become a quadruple junior champion in Italy's track cycling. In 2022, he won the European team pursuit championship and secured a podium spot in the individual pursuit at the European Elite Championships. He continued his winning streak by becoming the vice-world champion in team pursuit at the end of the year. In February 2023, he added another European team pursuit title to his impressive list of accomplishments.

Kristin Armstrong

Kristin Armstrong is an American cyclist specializing in time trials. She won three consecutive Olympic gold medals in the time trial at the 2008, 2012, and 2016 Olympic Games. She is also a multiple-time world champion in time trials.

Marianne Vos

Marianne Vos is a versatile Dutch cyclist. She has won multiple world championships in cyclo-cross, track, and road racing. She is also an Olympic gold medalist and has claimed victory in many prestigious races, including La Flèche Wallonne and the Tour of Flanders.

Eddy Merckx

Nicknamed "The Cannibal," Eddy Merckx is considered one of the greatest cyclists of all time. He won five Tours de France, five Giro d'Italia titles, one Vuelta a España, and numerous classic races, including Paris-Roubaix and Liège-Bastogne-Liège. His dominance in the 1960s and 1970s made him a cycling legend
